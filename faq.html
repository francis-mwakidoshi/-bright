How to calculate the number of pages to order?


A page is approximately 275 words. To calculate the number of pages to order for, simply divide the number of desired words with 275 to get the number of pages for your order. Note that in case you get decimal-point pages, say 3.46 pages, round of the next whole number, for this case, four pages.
